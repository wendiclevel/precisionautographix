Frequently Asked Questions

Fleet graphics, DOT compliance, and on-site installation — Baldwin & Mobile County, AL

Do I need a USDOT number in Alabama?

Yes — if your commercial vehicle crosses state lines and weighs over 10,001 lbs GVWR, or operates intrastate at over 26,001 lbs (Alabama ALEA threshold), you are required to display a USDOT number on both sides of the vehicle. Use our free DOT Compliance Checker to find out exactly what your vehicle needs.

Do you install on-site in Baldwin County?

Yes — on-site installation is our primary service model. We come to your location throughout Baldwin County and Mobile County so your vehicles stay on the road. No towing, no downtime. We also schedule installs outside this area with advance notice.

What areas do you serve?

Our primary service area covers Baldwin County and Mobile County — including Daphne, Fairhope, Foley, Gulf Shores, Orange Beach, Spanish Fort, and Mobile. Outside this area, we schedule with advance notice and a travel fee applies.

What materials do you use?

We use premium 3M, Avery, and Orafol vinyl — the same brands specified for DOT-compliant and commercial-grade applications. All graphics are precision-cut on professional equipment for clean edges and long-lasting adhesion.

Can you design vehicle graphics from scratch?

Yes. Our in-house design team will build production-ready artwork based on your logo, brand colors, and layout preferences. No existing design file required — just tell us what you need.

How much does fleet graphics cost?

Pricing depends on the number of vehicles, graphics complexity, and whether on-site installation is needed. We provide free quotes — submit your project details and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
